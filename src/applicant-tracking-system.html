APPLICANT TRACKING SYSTEM

FloCareer will help you in managing and tracking your Jobs and Candidates better. It will enable your HR teams to optimize their efforts and make hiring efficient and productive, FloCareer also provides options to manage your recruitment partners for comprehensive coverage of your sourcing options.
This applicant tracking suit takes care of profile duplicity check, reporting, internal communications etc.
Our structured reports and analytics give you much needed insight into which sourcing channel is most effective for you, what is the average turnaround time at every stage of hiring, keeping track of each candidate’s video recording, in-depth interview report and resume at one place – as well as simple way to share it with hiring team or end-clients.
Our Applicant Tracking System (ATS) and Recruiters Management System (RMS) enables you to keep track of which recruiting agency gave which resume, and ability to de-duplicate profiles.
